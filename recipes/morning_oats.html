Morning Oats

Description

Oats, a dish that no matter what culture you belong to, you must have tried atleast once in your life. Cultivated back in ancient China dating as back as 7000 B.C., oats are served as a fulfilling dish in millions of households today.

Ingredients

It is a fairly simple dish that is comprised of 3 main ingredients and a few other optional ingredients.
  • Milk
  • Oats
  • Sugar/ Honey
  • Fruits (optional)
  • Cinnamon powder (optional)

Steps

Generally, Oats are ready to eat in under 5 minutes depending on what ingredients you add to it and this is a key factor that has made it a food of choice for all the busy lifestyles that people lead nowadays.
  1. Take a bowl and add 1/3 cup of oats into it
  2. Add about 1 cup of milk
  3. Heat in a microwave oven for about 3 minutes at high
  4. Add 1 tbsp sugar
  5. Add 1/3 tbsp cinnamon powder
  6. Top it up with chopped fruits of your choice
  7. Voila! Your oats are ready!
