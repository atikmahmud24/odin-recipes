Lunch Omelette

Description

As astonishing as it may sound, omelettes, paired up with a few key ingredients can always be a great lunch.

Ingredients

For the ingredients, we'll try to not make it too fancy and keep it as easy as possible.
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Green Pepper (optional)
  • Cooking oil
  • Cheese (optional)

Steps

This is also a fairly quick meal to prepare as it is also ready-to-eat in under 7 minutes!
  1. Break 3 medium-sized eggs into a bowl
  2. Add 1/2 tbsp salt
  3. Mix it well with a whisk
  4. Heat up a pan and add about 2 tbsp oil
  5. Pour the egg mixture into the pan
  6. Let it cook for 3-5 minutes on medium heat
  7. Once it hardens, add green pepper and cheese (optional)
  8. Flip the egg and cook till it's ready to eat
